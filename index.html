Our Story


Since 2002, our beauty brand has been a symbol of self-expression and liberation. We embrace the unconventional, the daring, and the bold. From our inception, we've encouraged individuals to express themselves freely, breaking societal norms and embracing their uniqueness. We celebrate the wild, the quirky, and the avant-garde. Our brand is not just about cosmetics; it's about empowerment, creativity, and embracing one's true self without hesitation or apology. Join us on this journey of self-discovery and fearless self-expression.

What inspires us is a fusion of extravagance, strength, and unwavering determination. We're captivated by the audacity of those who dare to push the boundaries, who embrace the unconventional and flamboyant in their pursuit of self-expression. Our admiration extends to individuals who embody resilience and fortitude, who navigate life's challenges with grace and unwavering resolve. It's the relentless pursuit of excellence, the refusal to settle for mediocrity, that ignites our passion and fuels our creativity. We draw inspiration from the bold, the fearless, and the unapologetically authentic, as we strive to empower others to embrace their unique essence and unleash their full potential. In a world that often seeks conformity, we celebrate the outliers, the rebels, and the trailblazers who dare to defy convention and chart their own course. They inspire us to dream bigger, reach higher, and never lose sight of the extraordinary possibilities that await those who dare to dream.
